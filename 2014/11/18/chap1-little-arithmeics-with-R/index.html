Little arithmetics with R

  • As previously started in its most basic form R can be used as a simple calculator.Consider the following arithmetic operators:
    • Addition: +
    • Subtraction: -
    • Multiplication: *
    • Division: /
    • Exponentiation: ^
    • Modulo: %%
  • The last two might need some explaining:
    • The ^ operator raises the number to its left to the power of the number to its right: for exapmale 3^2 is 9.
    • The modulo returns the remainder of the division of the number to the left by the number on its right, for example 5 modulo 3 or 5%%3 is 2.
